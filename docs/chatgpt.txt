I am planning to open a hamburger stand and I would like you to suggest a menu. The hamburger stand will be a
quick service restaurant, specializing in hamburgers. It will also offer chicken nuggets, chicken sandwiches,
and onion rings


For a quick-service hamburger stand, the strongest menus are usually **focused, fast, and easy to execute consistently**. Rather than offering dozens of items, build around a small number of burgers with customizable toppings.

# Suggested Best-Selling Core Menu

If you're opening your first location, I'd simplify to:

**Burgers**

1. Classic Cheeseburger
2. Double Cheeseburger
3. Bacon Cheeseburger
4. Signature Stand Burger

**Chicken**
5. Crispy Chicken Sandwich
6. Spicy Chicken Sandwich
7. Chicken Nuggets

**Sides**
8. Fries
9. Onion Rings

**Desserts**
10. Three milkshake flavors

This keeps inventory manageable, speeds service, reduces food waste, and still gives customers enough variety.
The most profitable quick-service restaurants often succeed by doing a small number of items exceptionally well.
